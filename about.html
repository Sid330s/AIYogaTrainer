About AI Yoga Trainer

Welcome to AI Yoga Trainer! This project, created by Siddharth Kale in 2020, is a blend of Machine Learning and Web Development, inspired by the visual recognition models I explored during my internship.

Working on AI Yoga Trainer has been an incredible experience and a significant milestone in my learning journey. It marks my first deep dive into both Machine Learning and Web Development, opening doors to knowledge I hadn't imagined before.

At its core, AI Yoga Trainer leverages cutting-edge technologies like Pose.Net, powered by Google’s TensorFlow, and ml5.js, a versatile JavaScript library designed for in-browser Machine Learning. A significant part of my inspiration came from The Coding Train’s Pose Estimation tutorial series on YouTube.

I’m profoundly grateful to my mentors, family, and friends for their encouragement throughout this journey. A special thanks to my wonderful team, whose collaboration and support made this project all the more rewarding.
